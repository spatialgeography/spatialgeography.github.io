### India's Industries
* **Iron & Steel**: SAIL plants (Bhilai, Rourkela, Durgapur, Bokaro), Tata Steel (Jamshedpur), and RINL (Visakhapatnam).
* **Cotton Textiles**: Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Coimbatore, Surat — India's largest employer after agriculture.
* **IT & Software**: Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, and NOIDA — the global outsourcing hub.
* **Automobile Industry**: Gurgaon-Manesar, Pune, Chennai (Detroit of India), and Sanand.
* **Petrochemicals & Refining**: Jamnagar (world's largest refinery), Mathura, Haldia, and Mangalore.
* **Industrial Corridors**: Delhi-Mumbai (DMIC), Amritsar-Kolkata (AKIC), Chennai-Bengaluru (CBIC).
