#### Major World Religions and Distribution

| Religion | Origin | Holy Book | Key Regions |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Hinduism** | India | Vedas, Upanishads, Gita | India, Nepal, Bali |
| **Islam** | Arabia | Quran | Middle East, N. Africa, S. Asia, SE Asia |
| **Christianity** | Palestine | Bible | Europe, Americas, Australia |
| **Buddhism** | India (Bihar) | Tripitaka | SE Asia, East Asia, Sri Lanka |
| **Sikhism** | India (Punjab) | Guru Granth Sahib | Punjab, diaspora |
| **Jainism** | India | Agamas | Gujarat, Rajasthan |
| **Judaism** | Israel | Torah | Israel, USA |

#### Branches of Islam
- **Sunni** — majority; follow Caliphate tradition
- **Shia** — follow lineage of Ali; dominant in Iran, Iraq

#### Religious Diversity in India
- India — officially a **secular state*
- Major religions: Hinduism (~79.8%), Islam (~14.2%), Christianity (~2.3%), Sikhism (~1.7%), Buddhism (~0.7%), Jainism (~0.4%)
