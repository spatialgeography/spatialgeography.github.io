Urban growth, challenges, and planning.

::: {.callout-tip}
### Urban India
* **Census Definition**: Statutory (municipal bodies) and census towns (population/density/non-agricultural criteria).
* **Urban Growth Trends**: Million-plus cities — from 5 (1951) to 53+ (2021); megacities (Delhi, Mumbai).
* **Mega-Cities Population (2011 Census)**: Delhi > Mumbai > Kolkata > Chennai (descending order).
* **State-wise Level of Urbanisation (2011)**: Mizoram > Karnataka > Rajasthan > Uttar Pradesh (descending order).
* **Class I-VI Towns**: Census hierarchical classification of urban centers.
* **Urban Problems**: Housing shortage, slums, water supply, sanitation, traffic congestion, and air pollution.
* **Urban Planning**: Smart Cities Mission, AMRUT, PMAY, and HRIDAY.
* **Urban Governance**: Municipal corporations, municipal councils, and the 74th Constitutional Amendment.
:::

::: {.callout-note}
### Key India Facts (CUET PG)
* **Water Resources**: India accounts for about **4%** of the world's water resources.
* **Religious Composition (Census 2011)**: Hinduism (~79.8%), Islam (~14.2%), **Christianity (~2.3%)**, Sikhism (~1.7%), Buddhism (~0.7%), Jainism (~0.4%).
* **Coffee Production**: **Karnataka** is the leading coffee producing state (2020-21), followed by Kerala and Tamil Nadu.
* **Most Widely Distributed Industry**: The **Sugar industry** is the most widely distributed industry of India.
* **Per Capita Energy Consumption (Descending)**: Canada > USA > China > India.
* **Leading Marine Fisheries Country (2020, FAO 2022)**: **China** is the leading country in marine fisheries.
* **World's Leading Merchandise Importer (2022)**: **USA**.
:::
