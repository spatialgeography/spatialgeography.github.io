How agricultural land is owned, managed, and regulated.

### Land Management
* **Von Thünen's Model**: Agricultural land use as a function of distance from the market.
* **Land Tenure Systems**: Owner-cultivated, tenant farming, sharecropping, and cooperative farming.
* **Land Reforms in India**: Abolition of intermediaries, tenancy reforms, land ceiling legislation.
* **Land Degradation**: Soil erosion, salinization, waterlogging, and desertification.
* **Precision Agriculture**: GPS-guided farming, soil mapping, variable-rate technology, and drones.
:::
