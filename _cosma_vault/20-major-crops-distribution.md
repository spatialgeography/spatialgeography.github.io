### Crop Geography
* **Rice**: Monsoon Asia — India, China, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Vietnam.
* **Wheat**: Mid-latitude steppes and plains — USA, India, China, Russia, France.
* **Maize**: Americas, China, Africa — versatile cereal for food, feed, and biofuel.
* **Cotton**: Tropical and subtropical belts — India, USA, China, Pakistan, Brazil.
* **Tea & Coffee**:
  - **Coffee**: The coffee tree is native to the **highlands of Ethiopia** (not China). Contains a stimulant called **caffeine** (not tannic acid). **Mocha coffee** is grown chiefly in **Yemen**.
  - **Tea**: Different grades of tea are obtained at **varying altitudes** — higher altitudes produce superior quality. Major producers: India (Assam, Darjeeling), China, Kenya, Sri Lanka.
* **Sugarcane**: Tropical regions — Brazil, India, Thailand, China.
* **Oilseeds**: Soybean (USA, Brazil), Palm Oil (Indonesia, Malaysia), Groundnut (India, China).
