### Road, Rail, Water & Air
* **Road Transport**: Flexibility, last-mile connectivity, highway networks, and expressways.
* **Rail Transport**: Bulk freight, high-speed rail, trans-continental lines, and dedicated freight corridors.
* **Water Transport**: Inland waterways, maritime shipping, ports, and containerization.
* **Air Transport**: Hub-and-spoke models, international air routes, and logistics hubs.
* **Pipelines**: Crude oil, natural gas, and product pipeline networks.
:::
