### Industrial Classification
* **Agro-Based Industries**: Cotton textiles, jute, sugar, food processing — linked to agricultural raw materials.
* **Mineral-Based Industries**: Iron & steel, aluminum, cement, petrochemicals — linked to mineral deposits.
* **Light Industries**: Electronics, pharmaceuticals, garments — high value-to-weight ratio.
* **Heavy Industries**: Shipbuilding, heavy machinery, automobiles — large scale, capital-intensive.
* **Cottage & Small-Scale Industries**: Handicrafts, handloom, pottery — decentralized, labor-intensive.
* **Knowledge Industries**: IT, biotechnology, R&D — human capital as the primary resource.
:::
