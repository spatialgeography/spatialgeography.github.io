### Core Concepts
* **Accessibility & Connectivity**: Measuring how well places are connected to transport networks.
* **Complementarity & Transferability**: Ullman's bases for spatial interaction.
* **Distance Decay**: The diminishing intensity of interaction with increasing distance.
* **Freight vs. Passenger Transport**: Distinct spatial logics and economic considerations.
:::
