### Roads in India (NET Notes — Pulakesh Pradhan)

#### Key Road Development Timeline
| Year | Event |
|:---|:---|
| **1927** | Jayakar Committee (Dr. M.R. Jayakar) |
| **1934** | Indian Road Congress established; Nagpur Plan |
| **1950** | Central Road Research Institute (CRRI) established at New Delhi |

#### National Highway Numbering Rule
- **North–South highways:** Even numbers (2, 4, 6...); increases East to West
- **East–West highways:** Odd numbers (1, 3, 5...); increases North to South
- **Major highways:** Single or double digit

#### Major National Highways

| NH | Route |
|:---|:---|
| NH 1 | Delhi to Amritsar (via Ambala & Jalandhar) |
| NH 2 | Delhi to Kolkata |
| NH 3 | Agra to Mumbai |
| NH 4 | Thane (Mumbai) to Chennai |
| NH 7 | Varanasi to Kanyakumari |
| NH 8 | Delhi to Mumbai (via Jaipur, Ahmedabad, Baroda) |

#### Major Expressways of India
- **Mumbai–Pune Expressway** (Six-lane; 94 km)
- **Greater Noida–Agra / Yamuna Expressway** (NH 2)



### Railways in India (NET Notes)

#### Timeline
| Year | Event |
|:---|:---|
| 1832 | First railway proposal in Madras |
| 16 April 1853 | First passenger train: Bori Bunder (Mumbai) → Thane |
| 1871 | Calcutta, Bombay & Madras connected |

- **India — 4th largest railway network in the world*
- **Samjhauta Express** → Pakistan; **Maitri Express** → Bangladesh
