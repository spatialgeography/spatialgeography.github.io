### World Industrial Geography
* **North America**: Great Lakes / Rust Belt, Pacific Northwest, Silicon Valley, Gulf Coast petrochemical belt.
* **Europe**: Ruhr Valley (Germany), Midlands (UK), Paris Basin, Northern Italy (Third Italy).
* **East Asia**: Tokyo-Yokohama-Osaka (Japan), Pearl River Delta, Yangtze Delta, Seoul-Incheon (South Korea).
* **South Asia**: Mumbai-Pune, Ahmedabad, Chotanagpur (India), Karachi (Pakistan), Dhaka (Bangladesh garments).
* **Emerging Hubs**: Vietnam, Indonesia, Ethiopia, Mexico — the next wave of industrial relocation.
:::
