### Urban Dynamics
* **Drivers of Urbanization**: Rural-urban migration, natural increase, and reclassification.
* **Stages of Urbanization**: Pre-industrial, industrial, post-industrial urbanization models.
* **Suburbanization**: Outward expansion driven by transport, housing preference, and land cost.
* **Counter-urbanization**: Migration from cities to rural areas — causes and implications.
* **Urban Sprawl**: Unplanned, low-density expansion — environmental and social consequences.
* **Urbanization in India**: Census trends, million-plus cities, and regional disparities.
:::
