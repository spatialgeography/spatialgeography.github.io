## Ocean Trenches, Seas and Ridges — Reference

### Complete Ocean Trenches Reference (NET Notes — Pulakesh Pradhan)

> Total 57 deeps (Pacific = 32, Atlantic = 19, Indian = 6)
> Deepest: **Mariana Trench** = 11,034 m (36,070 feet)
> Exclusive Economic Zone = 12 to 200 nautical miles
> Deep Sea Plain = 75%+ of ocean floor; average depth 3000–6000 m

### Mid-Oceanic Ridge (NET Notes)

- Iceland arc — at margins of convergent boundary; roll-back effect
- Mid-Atlantic Ridge — rift valley 10 km wide, walls 3 km high
- **Islands along Mid-Atlantic Ridge** — highly prone to volcanic activity
