### Frequency Distribution — Detailed (NET Notes — Pulakesh Pradhan)

#### Histogram
- Bar chart where area of each bar = frequency of class
- No gaps between bars (unlike bar diagrams)

#### Ogive (Cumulative Frequency Curve)
- Less than ogive — cumulated from below
- More than ogive — cumulated from above
- **Median** = intersection of both ogives

#### Class Interval Selection
- Equal class intervals preferred
- Sturges' formula: k = 1 + 3.322 log N (k = number of classes, N = total frequency)
