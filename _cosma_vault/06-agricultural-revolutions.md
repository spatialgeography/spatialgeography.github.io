### Revolutions in Farming
* **First Agricultural Revolution (Neolithic)**: The transition from hunting-gathering to settled farming ~10,000 BCE.
* **Second Agricultural Revolution**: Mechanization, crop rotation, and enclosure movement in 18th-19th century Europe.
* **Green Revolution**: High-Yielding Varieties (HYVs), irrigation, chemical fertilizers, and pesticides — especially in India (1960s-70s).
* **Gene Revolution**: GMOs, biotechnology, and precision agriculture.
* **Evergreen Revolution**: Swaminathan's concept — productivity without ecological harm.
:::
