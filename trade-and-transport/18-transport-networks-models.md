### Spatial Models
* **Graph Theory in Transport**: Nodes, edges, connectivity index, and network density.
* **Gravity Model**: Predicting traffic flow between two locations based on size and distance.
* **Christaller's Central Place Theory**: Transport networks as a function of market hierarchies.
* **Taaffe, Morrill & Gould Model**: Evolution of transport networks in developing countries.
:::
