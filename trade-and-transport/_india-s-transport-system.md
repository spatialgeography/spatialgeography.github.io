### National Infrastructure
* **Indian Railways**: The world's fourth-largest railway network — zones, gauge systems, and dedicated freight corridors.
* **National Highways**: Golden Quadrilateral, North-South & East-West Corridors, Bharatmala Pariyojana.
* **Ports & Shipping**: Major and minor ports, Sagarmala Project, and coastal shipping.
* **Civil Aviation**: Airport expansion, UDAN scheme, and regional connectivity.
* **Inland Waterways**: National Waterway 1 (Ganga) and others under development.
:::
