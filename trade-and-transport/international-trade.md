### Trade Theory & Practice
* **Comparative Advantage** (Ricardo): Why nations trade based on relative production efficiency.
* **Heckscher-Ohlin Model**: Trade based on factor endowments (labor, capital, land).
* **Terms of Trade**: Export-import price ratios and their impact on developing nations.
* **Trade Organizations**: WTO, regional blocs (EU, ASEAN, NAFTA/USMCA, RCEP).
* **Free Trade vs. Protectionism**: Tariffs, quotas, subsidies, and trade wars.
