### Emerging Technologies
* **Google Earth Engine**: Cloud-based planetary-scale analysis.
* **Deep Learning for RS**: CNNs, U-Net, and transformer architectures for image segmentation.
* **UAV/Drone Remote Sensing**: Very high resolution, on-demand data acquisition.
* **SAR Interferometry (InSAR)**: Land subsidence and deformation monitoring.
* **STAC & Cloud-Native Geospatial**: Modern data cataloging and analysis-ready data formats.
:::
