### Domain Applications
* **Land Use / Land Cover Mapping**: Change detection and urban expansion monitoring.
* **Natural Hazard Mapping**: Flood, landslide, and drought risk assessment.
* **Agricultural Monitoring**: Crop health, yield estimation, and precision agriculture.
* **Urban Planning**: Site suitability, infrastructure planning, and smart city mapping.
* **Environmental Monitoring**: Deforestation tracking, water quality assessment, and carbon mapping.
