### Processing Workflows
* **Pre-processing**: Geometric correction, atmospheric correction, and radiometric calibration.
* **Image Enhancement**: Contrast stretching, histogram equalization, spatial filtering, and PCA.
* **Image Classification**: Supervised (Maximum Likelihood, SVM, Random Forest) and Unsupervised (K-Means, ISODATA) methods.
* **Accuracy Assessment**: Confusion matrix, Kappa statistic, overall accuracy, producer's and user's accuracy.
* **Spectral Indices**: NDVI, NDWI, NDBI, EVI, SAVI, and their applications.
:::
