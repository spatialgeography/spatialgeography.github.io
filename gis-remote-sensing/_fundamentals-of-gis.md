### GIS Foundations
* **What is GIS?**: A framework for capturing, managing, analyzing, and displaying spatial data.
* **Components of GIS**: Hardware, software, data, people, and methods.
* **Coordinate Systems & Projections**: Geographic (lat/long) vs. projected (UTM, State Plane) coordinate systems.
* **Map Scale & Generalization**: Principles governing cartographic representation at different scales.
