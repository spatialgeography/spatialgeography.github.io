### Electromagnetic Spectrum & Sensors
* **Electromagnetic Spectrum**: Visible, infrared, thermal, and microwave regions used in remote sensing.
* **Passive vs. Active Sensors**: Optical/thermal sensors vs. RADAR and LiDAR.
* **Spatial, Spectral, Temporal & Radiometric Resolution**: The four pillars of sensor capability.
* **Key Satellites**: Landsat, Sentinel, MODIS, ASTER, and commercial high-resolution platforms.
