### Core GIS Operations
* **Overlay Analysis**: Intersection, union, and identity operations for combining spatial datasets.
* **Buffer Analysis**: Proximity-based zones around features.
* **Network Analysis**: Shortest path, service area, and location-allocation modeling.
* **Interpolation**: IDW, Kriging, and Spline methods for estimating values at unsampled locations.
* **Terrain Analysis**: Slope, aspect, hillshade, viewshed, and watershed delineation from DEMs.
