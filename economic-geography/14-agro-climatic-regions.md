#### Scholars Who Made Attempts
- Randhawa (1958), Slampa (1968), Sengupta & Shasyuk (1967)

#### Planning Commission of India (1989)
- With National Remote Sensing Agency (NRSA)
- Devised **15 Agro-Climatic Regions*
- **Basis:** Soil type, rainfall, temperature, water resources

#### Objectives of Agro-Climatic Zoning
1. Broad demand–supply balance of major commodities at national level
2. Maximise net income of producers
3. Generate additional employment for landless labourers
4. Provide framework for scientific and sustainable use of natural resources
