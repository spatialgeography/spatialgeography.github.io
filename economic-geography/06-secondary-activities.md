### Industrial Geography
* **Types of Industries**: Heavy, light, cottage, and agro-based industries.
* **Industrial Location Factors**: Raw materials, labor, market, transport, capital, and government policy.
* **Major Industrial Regions**: Ruhr Valley, Great Lakes, Tokyo-Yokohama, Mumbai-Pune corridor.
* **Industrial Revolution**: Phases, spatial diffusion, and contemporary deindustrialization.
* **Special Economic Zones (SEZs)**: Purpose, global distribution, and impact on development.
