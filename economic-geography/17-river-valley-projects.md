#### Cauvery Water Dispute
- States: Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka and Pondicherry
- Started: **1892*
- **1924:** Krishnaraja Sagar Dam agreement
- **2007:** Supreme Court Award:
  - Tamil Nadu: **56.62%*
  - Karnataka: **36.48%*
  - Kerala: **4.05%*
  - Pondicherry: **0.94%*
