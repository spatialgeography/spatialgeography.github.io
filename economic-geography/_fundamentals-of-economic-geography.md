### Key Principles
* **Spatial Division of Labour**: Why different regions specialize in different economic activities.
* **Factors of Production**: Land, labor, capital, and enterprise in a spatial context.
* **Economic Sectors**: Primary (agriculture, mining), Secondary (manufacturing), Tertiary (services), Quaternary (knowledge).
* **Core-Periphery Model**: Uneven development between advanced core regions and dependent peripheries.
:::
