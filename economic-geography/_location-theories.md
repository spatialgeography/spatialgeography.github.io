### Classical Models
* **Von Thünen's Agricultural Model**: Concentric rings of land use around a central market.
* **Weber's Industrial Location Theory**: Minimum transport cost, labor cost, and agglomeration.
* **Christaller's Central Place Theory**: Hierarchical arrangement of market centers.
* **Lösch's Profit Maximization**: Modifying Christaller's model with demand and profit zones.
* **Perroux's Growth Pole Theory**: Economic growth radiating from key propulsive industries.
:::
