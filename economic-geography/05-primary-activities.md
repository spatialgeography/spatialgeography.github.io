### Resource-Based Economies
* **Agriculture**: Subsistence vs. commercial farming, types of agriculture (intensive, extensive, plantation, mixed).
* **Mining**: Metallic and non-metallic minerals, spatial distribution of mineral resources.
* **Fishing**: Marine and inland fisheries, aquaculture, and global fishing zones.
* **Forestry**: Types of forests, timber production, and non-timber forest products.
* **Pastoral Activities**: Nomadic herding, commercial ranching, and transhumance.
