Agricultural Geography

Spatial patterns of agriculture, crop systems, land use, and food security.

Author

Geography Team

Welcome to the Agricultural Geography module of Geography OpenCourseWare. This course examines the spatial organization of agriculture — including farming systems, crop distribution, land use patterns, agricultural revolutions, and contemporary challenges of food security.

UGC NET Syllabus

  • Land capability classification and Land Use Planning.
  • Cropping Pattern: Methods of delineating crop combination regions (Weaver, Doi and Rafiullah), Crop diversification.
  • Von Thunen’s Model of Land Use Planning.
  • Measurement and Determinants of Agricultural Productivity, Regional variations in Agricultural Productivity.
  • Agricultural Systems of the World.
