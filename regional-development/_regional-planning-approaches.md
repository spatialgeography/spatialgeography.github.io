### Planning Frameworks
* **Top-Down vs. Bottom-Up Planning**: Centralized government planning vs. community-based approaches.
* **Growth Centre Strategy**: Concentrating investment in selected locations to stimulate surrounding areas.
* **Integrated Area Development**: Multi-sectoral, holistic development of lagging regions.
* **Backward Area Development**: Special incentives and targeted programs for underdeveloped regions.
* **Participatory Planning**: Stakeholder engagement and democratic decision-making in regional policy.
:::
