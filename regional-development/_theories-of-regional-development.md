### Development Models
* **Rostow's Stages of Growth**: Traditional society to age of mass consumption.
* **Myrdal's Cumulative Causation**: Backwash and spread effects reinforcing regional inequality.
* **Hirschman's Unbalanced Growth**: Polarization and trickle-down effects in development.
* **Friedmann's Core-Periphery Model**: Spatial evolution of the economy from pre-industrial to post-industrial stages.
* **Perroux's Growth Pole Theory**: Development radiating from propulsive industries in key locations.
* **Dependency Theory**: Underdevelopment as a product of exploitative core-periphery relations.
:::
