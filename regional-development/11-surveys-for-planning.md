### Surveys for Planning (NET Notes — Pulakesh Pradhan)

#### Five Phases of Regional Planning
1. Survey
2. Projection
3. Policy formulation
4. Implementation
5. Assessment / Evaluation

#### Elements of Survey
- Regional analysis
- Regional diagnosis
- Regional prospecting

#### Types of Surveys

| Survey Type | Main Focus |
|:---|:---|
| **Regional Survey** | Regional analysis |
| **Diagnostic Survey** | Analysis + diagnosis; identifies deteriorated or deficient areas |
| **Techno-Economic Survey** | Regional perspective with focus on industrial/economic development |

#### Subject Matter of Regional Surveys
- **Place** — topography, drainage, geology, climate, soil, water, resources, energy
- **Folk** — demographic characteristics
- **Work** — economic potentialities

---

### Diagnostic Survey

- Identifies deteriorated or difficult/problem areas
- Discovers extent of deficiency
- Discovers people's attitudes toward deficiencies

#### Three Types of Problem Areas
1. **Redevelopment areas**
2. **Rehabilitation areas**
3. **Conservation areas**

---

### Techno-Economic Survey

- Conducted by **NCAER** — National Council of Applied Economic Research
- Focuses on industrial development
- **Bihar** selected for first techno-economic survey
