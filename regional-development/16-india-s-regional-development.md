### Indian Planning
* **Five Year Plans**: Historical evolution of India's approach to regional balance.
* **NITI Aayog**: Successor to the Planning Commission — cooperative and competitive federalism.
* **Special Category States**: North-Eastern states and hill states receiving enhanced development support.
* **Aspirational Districts Programme**: Targeting India's most underdeveloped districts for convergent action.
* **Industrial Corridors**: Delhi-Mumbai (DMIC), Chennai-Bengaluru, and Amritsar-Kolkata corridors.
* **North-East Development**: Look East/Act East Policy, connectivity, and border area development.
:::
