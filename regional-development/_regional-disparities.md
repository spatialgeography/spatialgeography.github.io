### Measuring Inequality
* **Inter-Regional Disparities**: Differences in income, employment, health, and education between regions.
* **Intra-Regional Disparities**: Inequality within regions — urban-rural divides.
* **Indicators of Development**: Per capita income, HDI, literacy, life expectancy, and poverty ratios.
* **Convergence vs. Divergence**: Whether regions are growing more similar or more different over time.
:::
