### Types of Regions
* **Formal (Uniform) Regions**: Defined by homogeneity in one or more characteristics (climate, language, soil type).
* **Functional (Nodal) Regions**: Organized around a central node — trade areas, commuting zones.
* **Planning (Administrative) Regions**: Defined for governance and policy purposes.
* **Perceptual (Vernacular) Regions**: Regions existing in popular culture and perception.
* **Regional Delineation Methods**: Statistical, cartographic, and GIS-based approaches.
:::
