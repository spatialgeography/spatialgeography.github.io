### Future of Cities
* **Smart Cities Mission (India)**: Digital infrastructure, e-governance, and sustainable urban services.
* **Transit-Oriented Development**: Dense, walkable communities centered on public transport.
* **Green Urbanism**: Urban forests, green roofs, water-sensitive urban design.
* **Slum Upgrading**: In-situ development, affordable housing, and inclusive urbanization.
* **Urban Resilience**: Climate-adaptive cities, disaster preparedness, and infrastructure redundancy.
:::
