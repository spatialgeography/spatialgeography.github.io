### Urban Structure Models
* **Burgess Concentric Zone Model**: City as concentric rings expanding from the CBD.
* **Hoyt Sector Model**: Wedge-shaped sectors along transport corridors.
* **Harris & Ullman Multiple Nuclei Model**: City organized around several distinct nuclei.
* **Indian Urban Morphology**: Pre-colonial, colonial, and post-colonial city forms.
* **Urban Land Use Zoning**: Residential, commercial, industrial, and recreational zones.
