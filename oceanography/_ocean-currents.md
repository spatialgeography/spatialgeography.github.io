### Global Current Systems
* **Surface Currents**: Wind-driven gyres — North Atlantic, South Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Ocean systems.
* **Warm Currents**: Gulf Stream, Kuroshio, Brazil Current — moderating high-latitude climates.
* **Cold Currents**: Labrador, Canary, Benguela, Humboldt — creating coastal aridity and upwelling.
* **Upwelling & Downwelling**: Nutrient-rich cold water rising to the surface; implications for fisheries.
* **El Niño & La Niña**: Disruption of normal Pacific current patterns with global climate impacts.
:::
