### Wave & Tidal Dynamics
* **Wave Generation**: Wind speed, duration, and fetch controlling wave characteristics.
* **Wave Erosion & Deposition**: Coastal landform creation through wave action.
* **Tides**: Gravitational pull of the Moon and Sun — spring and neap tides.
* **Tidal Patterns**: Diurnal, semi-diurnal, and mixed tidal regimes.
* **Tidal Energy**: Harnessing tidal range and tidal currents for power generation.
:::
