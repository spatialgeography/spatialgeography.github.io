### Ocean Water Properties
* **Temperature Distribution**: Horizontal (latitude-based) and vertical (thermocline) patterns.
* **Salinity**: Average 35 ppt — influenced by evaporation, precipitation, river input, and ice formation.
* **Thermohaline Circulation**: Density-driven deep ocean circulation connecting the global ocean system.
* **Water Masses**: Identification and tracking of distinct water bodies by their T-S characteristics.
