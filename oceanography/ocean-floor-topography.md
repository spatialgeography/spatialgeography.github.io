### Submarine Relief Features
* **Continental Shelf**: Shallow, gently sloping extension of continents — rich in marine resources.
* **Continental Slope**: Steep transition zone between the shelf and deep ocean floor.
* **Abyssal Plains**: Flat, deep-ocean floor covered in fine sediments.
* **Mid-Ocean Ridges**: The longest mountain chains on Earth — sites of seafloor spreading.
* **Ocean Trenches**: The deepest zones — subduction boundaries (Mariana Trench, 11,034 m).
* **Seamounts & Guyots**: Volcanic peaks rising from the ocean floor.
:::
