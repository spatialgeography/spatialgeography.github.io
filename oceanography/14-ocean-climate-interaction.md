### Climate-Ocean Coupling
* **Heat Transport**: Oceans as the planet's primary heat reservoirs and redistributors.
* **Carbon Cycle**: Oceanic CO₂ absorption, biological pump, and ocean acidification.
* **Sea Level Rise**: Thermal expansion and ice sheet melt — projected impacts.
* **Indian Ocean Dipole (IOD)**: Regional climate oscillation affecting the Indian subcontinent.
:::
