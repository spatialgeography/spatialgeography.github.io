### Oceanic Resources
* **Marine Fisheries**: Major fishing grounds, sustainable yield, and overfishing concerns.
* **Mineral Resources**: Manganese nodules, polymetallic sulfides, and rare earth elements.
* **Petroleum & Natural Gas**: Offshore exploration in continental shelf regions.
* **Marine Biodiversity**: Coral reefs, mangroves, and deep-sea ecosystems.
* **Maritime Boundaries**: Territorial seas, EEZ, continental shelf limits (UNCLOS).
