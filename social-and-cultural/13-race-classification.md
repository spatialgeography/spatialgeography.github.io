### Race (NET Notes — Pulakesh Pradhan)

> **Race** — the similarities of genetic patterns among aggregates of individuals of human populations

#### Two Types of Genes
- **Dominant genes**
- **Recessive genes**

#### Two Major Classification Systems

| System | Basis |
|:---|:---|
| **Phenotype** | Physical features (skin colour, etc.) |
| **Genotype** | Genetic origin of physical traits |

#### Blood Group Distribution

| Blood Group | Common In |
|:---|:---|
| **Type B** | Central and South-East Asia |
| **Type O** | Western Europeans, Australians, North-East Asia |

### Major Racial Types

#### 1. Negroids
- Dark black skin
- Dark eyes
- Thick and averted lips
- Prognathous jaw
- Black woolly hair
- Broad and flat nose
- Long head
- Stocky body build

#### 2. Mongoloids
- Light yellow or brown skin
- Straight and coarse black hair
- Broad head
- High cheekbones
- Short but stocky build
- Brown eyes
- Flat face and nose
- Epicanthic eye-fold

#### 3. Caucasoids
- Fair skin and eyes
- Prominent and narrow nose
- Abundant body hair
- Light and wavy hair
- Thin lips
- **Three subgroups:**
  1. Nordic
  2. Alpine
  3. Mediterranean

#### 4. Negritos / Negrilloids
- Yellow to brown skin
- Black spiral hair
- Short stature
- Varying blood groups
- **Negrillo** — Congo basin

#### 5. Bushmanoids
- Flat face
- Epicanthic eye fold

#### 6. Australoids
- Dark skin and eyes
- Dark wavy hair
- Broad nose
- Full lips
- Long head
- **Blood groups: 'A' and 'O'**
- Examples in India: **Bhil** of Deccan Plateau; **Oraon** of Chotanagpur Plateau

#### 7. Papuan Melanesians
- Frizzy hair
- Regions: New Guinea and Melanesia
