### Reading Landscapes
* **Carl Sauer's Cultural Landscape**: Culture as an agent shaping the natural landscape.
* **Vernacular Architecture**: Building styles reflecting climate, materials, and cultural traditions.
* **Agricultural Landscapes**: Terraced fields, paddy landscapes, pastoral scenes, and plantation estates.
* **Urban Cultural Landscapes**: Markets, heritage zones, religious quarters, and cosmopolitan districts.
* **UNESCO World Heritage Sites**: Cultural and mixed-heritage sites as expressions of geographic identity.
