### The German & French Schools
* **Alexander von Humboldt**: Systematic physical geography, *Kosmos*, and the holistic view of nature.
* **Carl Ritter**: Teleological approach and regional comparative geography.
* **Friedrich Ratzel**: Anthropogeography, environmental determinism, and political geography.
* **Paul Vidal de la Blache**: French school of *genre de vie*, possibilism, and regional monographs.
* **Determinism vs. Possibilism**: The foundational debate shaping modern geographic methodology.
:::
