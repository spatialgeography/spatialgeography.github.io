## Isolines Reference

### Complete Isoline Reference (NET Notes — Pulakesh Pradhan)

> A contour line is a curve along which a function has a constant value. Contour lines often have names beginning with **iso**, meaning equal.



#### Pressure and Atmosphere

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isobar** | Line of equal pressure |
| **Isallobar** | Line of equal pressure change during a specific time interval |
| **Isobaric** | Constant pressure |



#### Temperature

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isotherm** | Equal temperature |
| **Isogeotherm** | Equal mean annual temperature |
| **Isocheim** | Equal mean winter temperature |
| **Isothere** | Equal mean summer temperature |
| **Isohel** | Equal or constant solar radiation |



#### Rainfall and Humidity

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isohyet** | Equal rainfall line |
| **Isohume** | Line of constant relative humidity |
| **Isoneph** | Equal cloud cover line |
| **Isochalaz** | Line of constant frequency of hail storms |
| **Isobront** | Line through places where thunderstorm activity occurred simultaneously |



#### Wind and Movement

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isotach** | Line joining points with constant wind speed |
| **Isopectic** | Equal dates of the formation of winter |



#### Elevation and Depth

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isohyght / Isohypse** | Line of constant geopotential height on a constant pressure surface |
| **Isopycnal** | Line joining constant density |
| **Isobathytherm** | Line of equal depth of water with equal temperature |
| **Isopaches** | Equal thickness |



#### Ocean and Water

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isohalines** | Lines joining equal ocean salinity |
| **Isoplat** | Acid precipitation indicated on a map |



#### Magnetism

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isogonic line** | Line of constant magnetic declination |



#### Economics and Transport

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isochrone** | Lines of equivalent drive time or travel time to a given location |
| **Isotim** | Equivalent transport costs from the source of a raw material |
| **Isodapane** | Equivalent costs of travel time |
| **Isoquant** | Curve of equal production quantity for alternative combinations |



#### Biology

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isoflor** | Areas of comparable biological diversity |



#### General

| Isoline | Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Isopleth** | Contours for variables that cannot be measured at a point and must be calculated over an area (e.g., population density) |
| **Isochons** | Constant specific volume |
