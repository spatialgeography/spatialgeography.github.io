### 21st Century Industry
* **Deindustrialization**: Decline of traditional manufacturing in the Global North — the Rust Belt, Northern England.
* **Reindustrialization**: Reshoring, nearshoring, and the return of manufacturing to advanced economies.
* **Industry 4.0**: Automation, IoT, AI, and smart manufacturing — the fourth industrial revolution.
* **Global Value Chains (GVCs)**: Fragmented production across borders — design in one country, assembly in another.
* **Circular Economy**: Waste minimization, recycling, and cradle-to-cradle industrial design.
* **Green Industry**: Low-carbon manufacturing, renewable energy equipment, and environmental compliance.
:::
