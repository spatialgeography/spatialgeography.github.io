### Current Challenges
* **Global Food Security**: FAO definition — availability, access, utilization, and stability.
* **Hunger & Malnutrition**: Global Hunger Index, stunting, wasting, and micronutrient deficiency.
* **India's Food Security**: PDS, NFSA (2013), PM-KISAN, and buffer stock management.
* **Climate Change & Agriculture**: Shifting agro-climatic zones, crop failure risk, and adaptation strategies.
* **WTO & Agriculture**: Agreement on Agriculture, subsidies, market access, and developing country concerns.
* **Organic Farming**: Principles, certification, and the growth of sustainable agriculture.
:::
