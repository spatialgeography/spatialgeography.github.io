### Political Entities
* **State vs. Nation**: Territorial sovereignty vs. cultural identity; nation-states and multi-nation states.
* **Components of a State**: Territory, population, government, and sovereignty.
* **Centripetal & Centrifugal Forces**: Forces that unite or divide a state (nationalism, ethnic tension, federalism).
* **Failed States & Separatism**: State collapse, secessionist movements, and contested territories.
* **Federal vs. Unitary States**: Spatial distribution of political authority.
:::
