### Border Dynamics
* **Types of Boundaries**: Natural (rivers, mountains), geometric (latitude/longitude lines), and ethnographic.
* **Boundary Evolution**: Antecedent, subsequent, superimposed, and relict boundaries.
* **Boundary Disputes**: Territorial, positional, resource, and functional disputes.
* **Frontiers vs. Boundaries**: Zones of transition vs. lines of demarcation.
* **India's Borders**: McMahon Line, Radcliffe Line, Line of Control, and Durand Line.
:::
