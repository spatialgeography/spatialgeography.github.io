### Spatial Dimensions of Democracy
* **Electoral Systems**: First-past-the-post, proportional representation, and mixed systems.
* **Gerrymandering**: Manipulating constituency boundaries for political advantage.
* **Voting Patterns**: Urban-rural divides, regional party strongholds, and swing regions.
* **Indian Electoral Geography**: Constituency delimitation, regional parties, and coalition politics.
