### Classical & Modern Geopolitics
* **Mackinder's Heartland Theory**: Whoever controls East Europe commands the Heartland, and thereby the World Island.
* **Spykman's Rimland Theory**: The coastal periphery as the key to global dominance.
* **Mahan's Sea Power Theory**: Naval strength as the foundation of national power.
* **Cohen's Geostrategic Regions**: Shatterbelts and the hierarchical structure of global geopolitics.
* **Critical Geopolitics**: Deconstructing geopolitical discourse and the power of representation.
:::
