## Geopolitics and Global Strategic Views

### 1. Karl Haushofer and Geopolitik
*   Borrowed the term from **Rudolf Kjellén**.
*   In 1924, Haushofer began editing the journal *"Zeitschrift fur Geopolitik"*.
*   Established **"The Institut"** in Munich, which focused on the dependence of political events upon the "soil" (earth).

### 2. Saul Cohen: World Divided (1973)
*   In his book *"Geography and Politics in a World Divided"*, Cohen offered a hierarchical and regional world model.
*   He identified two types of regions:
    1.  **Geostrategic Regions**: Large-scale global divisions.
    2.  **Geopolitical Regions**: Subdivisions within geostrategic regions.
*   Cohen's model allowed for more traditional geographical concepts to be integrated into modern global strategic thinking.
