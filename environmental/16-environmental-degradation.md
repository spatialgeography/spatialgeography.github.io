### Degradation Processes
* **Deforestation**: Causes, rates, and consequences — Amazon, Congo Basin, Southeast Asia.
* **Soil Degradation**: Erosion, salinization, waterlogging, and desertification.
* **Water Pollution**: Point and non-point source pollution, eutrophication, and groundwater contamination.
* **Air Pollution**: Sources, types of pollutants, smog, acid rain, and particulate matter.
* **Biodiversity Loss**: Habitat destruction, overexploitation, invasive species, and the sixth mass extinction.
