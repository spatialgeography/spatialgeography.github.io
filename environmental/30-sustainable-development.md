### Sustainability Frameworks
* **Brundtland Definition**: "Meeting the needs of the present without compromising future generations."
* **SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals)**: The 17 UN goals — a geographic perspective.
* **Ecological Footprint**: Measuring human demand on Earth's ecosystems.
* **Circular Economy**: Waste reduction, resource efficiency, and cradle-to-cradle design.
* **Green Technology**: Renewable energy, green infrastructure, and sustainable urban design.
:::
