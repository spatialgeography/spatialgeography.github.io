### Hazard Assessment
* **Earthquakes**: Seismic zones, plate boundaries, and earthquake-prone regions of India.
* **Volcanic Eruptions**: Types of volcanoes, eruption styles, and volcanic hazard zones.
* **Floods**: Fluvial, pluvial, and coastal flooding — causes, frequency, and mitigation.
* **Cyclones**: Tropical cyclone formation, tracks, intensity scales, and warning systems.
* **Droughts**: Meteorological, hydrological, and agricultural droughts — vulnerability mapping.
* **Landslides**: Trigger factors, susceptibility mapping, and early warning.
