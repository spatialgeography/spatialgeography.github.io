### Distribution Patterns
* **Island Biogeography Theory** (MacArthur & Wilson): Species richness as a function of island area and isolation.
* **Zoogeographic Realms**: Palaearctic, Nearctic, Neotropical, Ethiopian, Oriental, and Australasian.
* **Floristic Kingdoms**: Boreal, Palaeotropical, Neotropical, South African, Australian, and Antarctic.
* **Endemism & Hotspots**: Biodiversity hotspots — Western Ghats, Eastern Himalayas, Sundaland.
* **Migration & Dispersal**: Animal migration routes and barriers to species dispersal.
