### Policy Frameworks
* **Stockholm Conference (1972)**: The beginning of global environmental consciousness.
* **Rio Earth Summit (1992)**: Agenda 21, CBD, UNFCCC, and the principle of common but differentiated responsibilities.
* **Kyoto Protocol & Paris Agreement**: Climate change mitigation frameworks and nationally determined contributions.
* **Indian Environmental Policy**: Wildlife Protection Act, Forest Conservation Act, EIA notifications, National Action Plan on Climate Change.
* **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)**: Process, stages, and significance in project planning.
:::
