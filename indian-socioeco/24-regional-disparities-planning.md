### Development Planning
* **Inter-State Disparities**: Per capita income variation — Goa, Kerala, Tamil Nadu vs. Bihar, UP, Jharkhand.
* **HDI Ranking**: State-wise Human Development Index — education, health, and income dimensions.
* **Backward Region Development**: BRGF, Aspirational Districts, and hill area development programmes.
* **Border Area Development**: BADP for India's peripheral and strategically important border regions.
* **North-East India**: Special economic and development challenges — Look East / Act East Policy.
* **NITI Aayog SDG Index**: Tracking India's progress on Sustainable Development Goals by state.
:::
