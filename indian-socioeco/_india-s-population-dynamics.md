### Demographic Profile
* **Population Size & Growth**: From 361 million (1951) to 1.4 billion — trends, projections, and demographic dividend.
* **Density & Distribution**: Bihar, West Bengal, and Kerala vs. Arunachal Pradesh and Mizoram.
* **Sex Ratio**: Child sex ratio concerns — Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan vs. Kerala, Meghalaya.
* **Literacy**: Interstate variations — Kerala (96%) vs. Bihar (62%) — gender and rural-urban gaps.
* **Religious & Linguistic Diversity**: Scheduled languages, religious distribution, and the linguistic map of India.
* **Tribal Population**: Scheduled Tribes — distribution, development, and rights (PESA, FRA).
