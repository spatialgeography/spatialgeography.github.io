### Soil Types
* **Alluvial Soil**: Old (Bangar) and New (Khadar) — most extensive and agriculturally productive.
* **Black Soil (Regur)**: Cotton soil of the Deccan — rich in montmorillonite clay, self-plowing character.
* **Red & Yellow Soils**: Formed by weathering of crystalline rocks — lateritic iron and aluminum oxides.
* **Laterite Soil**: Heavily leached, high iron content — Kerala, Karnataka, and Meghalaya.
* **Forest & Mountain Soils**: Thin, acidic soils of the Himalayan and Western Ghat slopes.
* **Arid & Desert Soils**: Sandy, low humus, high salt content — Rajasthan and Gujarat.
:::
