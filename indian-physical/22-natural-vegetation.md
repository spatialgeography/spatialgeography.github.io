### Vegetation Types
* **Tropical Evergreen**: Dense, multi-layered canopy — Western Ghats, Andaman Islands, Northeast India (>200 cm rainfall).
* **Tropical Deciduous**: India's most extensive forest type — Teak, Sal, and bamboo-dominated.
* **Tropical Thorn**: Scrub and xerophytic species — Rajasthan, interior Deccan.
* **Montane Forests**: Successional vegetation from subtropical broadleaf to alpine meadows.
* **Mangroves**: Sundarbans, Bhitarkanika, and Gujarat's coastal mangrove systems.
* **Biodiversity Hotspots**: Western Ghats and Eastern Himalayas.
:::
