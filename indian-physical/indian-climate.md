### Monsoon Dynamics
* **Monsoon Mechanism**: Differential heating, ITCZ shift, Somali Jet, Tibetan High, and El Niño influence.
* **Indian Seasons**: Winter (Jan–Feb), Summer (Mar–May), Advancing Monsoon (Jun–Sep), Retreating Monsoon (Oct–Nov).
* **Rainfall Distribution**: Spatial variation — Cherrapunji/Mawsynram vs. Thar Desert.
* **Tropical Cyclones**: Bay of Bengal cyclogenesis, Saffir-Simpson scale, and disaster preparedness.
* **Climate Zones of India**: Tropical wet, tropical dry, subtropical humid, semi-arid, arid, and montane.
:::
