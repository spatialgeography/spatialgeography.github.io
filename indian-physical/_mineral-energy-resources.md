### Resource Geography
* **Metallic Minerals**: Iron ore (Jharkhand, Odisha), bauxite (Odisha, Gujarat), manganese, copper, and chromite.
* **Non-Metallic Minerals**: Mica, limestone, gypsum, and dolomite.
* **Coal**: Gondwana (Jharia, Raniganj, Singrauli) and Tertiary coalfields (Meghalaya).
* **Petroleum & Natural Gas**: Mumbai High, Bassein, KG Basin, and Gujarat fields.
* **Renewable Energy**: Solar (Rajasthan, Gujarat), wind (Tamil Nadu, Gujarat), and hydropower potential.
:::
