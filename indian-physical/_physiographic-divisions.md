### Landform Regions
* **The Himalayas**: Greater, Lesser, and Outer Himalayas — structure, formation, and regional variation (Kashmir, Kumaon, Nepal, Assam).
* **The Northern Plains**: Indo-Gangetic plain — Bhabar, Terai, Bangar, and Khadar zones.
* **The Peninsular Plateau**: Deccan Plateau, Malwa Plateau, Chota Nagpur Plateau — ancient crystalline shield.
* **The Coastal Plains**: Western (Konkan, Malabar) and Eastern (Coromandel, Northern Circars) coastal strips.
* **The Islands**: Andaman & Nicobar (volcanic origin) and Lakshadweep (coral atolls).
* **The Thar Desert**: Arid landscape of western Rajasthan — sand dunes, playas, and rocky hamada.
:::
