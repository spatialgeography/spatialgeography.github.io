### Growth Patterns
* **World Population Growth**: From 1 billion (1800) to 8 billion (2022) — the demographic explosion.
* **Components of Growth**: Birth rate (CBR), death rate (CDR), and natural increase (RNI).
* **Fertility Measures**: Total Fertility Rate (TFR), General Fertility Rate, and replacement-level fertility.
* **Mortality Measures**: Infant Mortality Rate (IMR), Maternal Mortality Ratio, and life expectancy.
* **Doubling Time**: Time required for a population to double at current growth rates.
* **India's Population Growth**: Census trends — from 361 million (1951) to 1.4 billion (2021).
:::
