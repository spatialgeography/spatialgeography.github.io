### Transition Stages
* **Stage 1 (Pre-Transition)**: High birth rates, high death rates — slow growth (pre-industrial societies).
* **Stage 2 (Early Transition)**: High births, declining deaths — rapid growth (improving healthcare/sanitation).
* **Stage 3 (Late Transition)**: Declining births, low deaths — decelerating growth (industrialization/urbanization).
* **Stage 4 (Post-Transition)**: Low births, low deaths — near-zero growth (developed nations).
* **Stage 5 (Decline)**: Sub-replacement fertility — negative natural increase (Japan, Germany, Italy).
* **India's Position**: States at different transition stages — Kerala (Stage 4) vs. Bihar (Stage 2-3).
:::
