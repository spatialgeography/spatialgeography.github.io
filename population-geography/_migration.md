### Migration Dynamics
* **Types of Migration**: Internal (rural-urban, intra-state) and international (emigration, immigration).
* **Push & Pull Factors**: Economic opportunity, safety, education, environment, and family.
* **Ravenstein's Laws of Migration**: Classic generalizations about migration patterns.
* **Lee's Theory of Migration**: Intervening obstacles, personal factors, and origin-destination dynamics.
* **Refugees & Forced Migration**: Conflict, persecution, and environmental displacement.
* **Indian Migration Patterns**: Rural-urban migration, seasonal labor migration, and the Gulf diaspora.
:::
