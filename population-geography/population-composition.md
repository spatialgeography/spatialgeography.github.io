### Demographic Structure
* **Age-Sex Structure**: Population pyramids — expansive, constrictive, and stationary types.

* **Sex Ratio**: Gender imbalance — global patterns and India's declining child sex ratio.
* **Literacy & Education**: Geographic variation in literacy rates and their development implications.
* **Occupational Structure**: Primary-, secondary-, and tertiary-sector workforce distribution.
* **Ethnic & Linguistic Composition**: Diversity, plurality, and the geography of languages and religions.
