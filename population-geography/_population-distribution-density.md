### Spatial Patterns
* **World Population Distribution**: Dense clusters in East Asia, South Asia, and Europe; sparse in deserts, polar, and high mountain regions.
* **Factors Affecting Distribution**: Climate, terrain, soil fertility, water availability, accessibility, and historical factors.
* **Population Density**: Arithmetic, physiological, and agricultural density — and what they reveal.
* **Ecumene & Non-Ecumene**: Habitable vs. uninhabitable areas of the Earth's surface.
