### Bivariate & Multivariate Analysis
* **Pearson's r & Spearman's rho**: Measuring linear and monotonic relationships.
* **Simple Linear Regression**: Predicting one variable from another.
* **Multiple Regression**: Incorporating several predictor variables.
* **Logistic Regression**: Binary outcome prediction (e.g., land cover presence/absence).
* **Residual Analysis**: Checking model assumptions and spatial autocorrelation in residuals.
:::
