### Climate in Practice
* **Agricultural Climatology**: Crop-weather relationships, growing degree days, and agro-climatic zones.
* **Urban Climatology**: Urban heat islands, air quality, and microclimate modification.
* **Weather Forecasting**: Synoptic charts, weather models, and satellite meteorology.
* **Disaster Risk**: Cyclones, droughts, floods, heat waves, and early warning systems.
:::
