### Global Climate Dynamics
* **Paleoclimatology**: Ice cores, tree rings, ocean sediments, and reconstructing past climates.
* **Greenhouse Effect & Global Warming**: Enhanced greenhouse forcing from CO₂, CH₄, and N₂O.
* **IPCC Assessment Reports**: Key findings, emission scenarios (RCPs/SSPs), and projected impacts.
* **El Niño & La Niña (ENSO)**: Pacific Ocean-atmosphere coupling and its global climate influence.
* **Ozone Depletion**: Causes, the Montreal Protocol, and the Antarctic ozone hole.
:::
