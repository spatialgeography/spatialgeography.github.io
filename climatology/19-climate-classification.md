### Classification Systems
* **Köppen System**: The most widely used system — Af, Am, Aw, BSh, BWh, Cfa, Cfb, Dfa, ET, EF.
* **Thornthwaite System**: Based on potential evapotranspiration and moisture index.
* **Trewartha Modification**: Refined Köppen boundaries for better representation.
* **Climatic Regions of India**: Tropical, subtropical, arid, semi-arid, and montane zones.
