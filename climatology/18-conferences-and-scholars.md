## International Conferences on Climate

| Conference / Protocol | Year | Key Outcome |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Vienna Conference** | 1985 | First to discuss ozone depletion |
| **Montreal Protocol** | 1987 | Phase out CFCs |
| **Helsinki Conference** | 1989 | Strengthened Montreal Protocol |
| **Kyoto Protocol** | 1997 | 141 countries; reduce greenhouse gas emissions |
| **GARP** | — | Global Atmospheric Research Programme |
| **MONEX** | 1979 | Monsoon Experiment; 104 aircraft missions |
| **ISMEX** | 1973–74 | Indo-Soviet Monsoon Experiment |
